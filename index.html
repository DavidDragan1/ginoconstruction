About Us

"At Gino Construction, we are committed to transforming spaces and building strong foundations for the future."

With over 20 years of experience in the construction industry, we specialise in a wide range of services, from house refurbishments and property extensions to complete structural alterations and custom-built garden rooms.

Our team of skilled professionals brings dedication, precision, and an eye for detail to every project, no matter the scale. Whether you are looking to enhance the comfort of your home, renovate your commercial space, or create the perfect outdoor sanctuary, Gino Construction is your trusted partner in bringing your vision to life.
